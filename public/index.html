Crossmint x BVP

Eager to join you in building an industry-defining company.

Rodri and Alfonso— it's been a pleasure getting to know you both. The BVP team has come away from our conversations blown away by your persistence, grit, thoughtfulness, and scrappiness.

Crossmint's vision is extremely compelling. We believe in NFTs as a fixture of commerce in web3, and are excited about applications to other fast-growing verticals including gaming. We're convinced that wallets are at the core of the web3 ecosystem and experience, and companies that own and manage wallet infra are well positioned to win. Early adopters of the product we talked to were abuzz about the core credit card check-out flow and excited about prepay, cross-chain payments, and products to come.

We love the opportunity to truly partner and build alongside our founders. To that end, the BVP team integrated and tested your product - and we were deeply impressed.

Let's give it a pass...
